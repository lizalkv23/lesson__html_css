Spider-Man

Peter Benjamin Parker is an ordinary kid who was once bitten by a radioactive spider, after which at school he discovered in himself the power and abilities similar to spiders. Soon he was able to crawl on walls and sense danger, and later even invented his own web and a device for emitting it to move around on it. Thor

Thor Odinson is the God of Thunder, a former king and prince of Asgard and a former member of the Avengers team. When his irresponsible actions led to a threat to his home world, Thor was stripped of his powers and banished to Earth by his father Odin, king of Asgard. While in exile, Thor learned humility, fell in love with Dr. Jane Foster, and saved his new friends from a destructive threat sent by his envious half-brother Loki, who dreams of seizing the throne of Asgard. Thor was able to stop Loki, as a result of which he was forgiven by Odin and received his power back.
